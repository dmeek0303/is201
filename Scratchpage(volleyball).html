Fun Volleyball Facts

  1. History of Volleyball
    • Invented in 1895 by William G. Morgan
    • Originally called "Mintonette"
    • Became an Olympic sport in 1964
  2. Basic Rules
    • Each team has 6 players on the court
    • Matches are played best of 5 sets
    • Rally scoring is used in modern games
  3. Types of Hits
    • Serve
    • Set
    • Spike
    • Block
    • Dig
